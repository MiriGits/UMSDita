Requisition States

A list of possible requsition states.

TypeDescription
Created Your order has passed through all budget checks and is currently in the process of becoming a PO.
Pending An order of over $100 that is waiting for your approver to authorize before going on to the next stage of budget checks.
Approved An order of under $100 that has become a requisition and is moving onto the next stage of budget checks.
Dispatched Your order has passed all budget checks, become a requistion, and is now an active PO.
Error Requistion could not be created due to an issue with your EFS chart string.
